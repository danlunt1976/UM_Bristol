## Abstract

This poster presents emission-driven HadCM3 simulations reconstructing the carbon cycle from the preindustrial period to the modern era. By driving the model with historical CO₂ emissions rather than prescribed concentrations, we assess how well HadCM3 captures the evolution of atmospheric CO₂ and associated land and ocean carbon fluxes over the historical period, providing a benchmark relevant to CMIP7.
